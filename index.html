Biography

I'm an associate professor in Department of Computer Science and Technology, Ocean University of China, and a member of the Institute of Artificial Intelligence. I earned my Ph.D. in Computer Science and Technology from Shandong University in 2020, supervised by Prof. Changhe Tu. My research interest is mainly focused on computer graphics and geometry processing, particularly on ​​AI-powered​​ topics in these fields recently.

Teaching

  • Undergraduate courses: Computer Graphics/ Design and Analysis of Algorithms.
  • Graduate courses: Analysis and Design of Algorithms/ Design Patterns.
