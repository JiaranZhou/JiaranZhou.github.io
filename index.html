Biography

I'm a lecturer in the Center on Artificial Intelligence, at Ocean University of China. I earned my Ph.D. in Computer Science and Technology from Shandong University in 2020, supervised by Prof. Changhe Tu. My research interest is mainly focused on computer graphics and geometry processing.

News

  • 2020.07: I will start working at Ocean Univeristy of China since this summer.
  • 2020.05: Our paper "Combinatorial Construction of Seamless Parameter Domains" won the Honorable Mention of Günter Enderle Award in Eurographics 2020(top 6%).
  • 2019.12: One paper was conditionally accepted to Eurographics 2020.
  • 2019.07: One paper was accepted by ACM Transactions on Graphics.

Experience

  • 2015.10–2017.10: Visiting student at New York University, supervised by Prof. Claudio T. Silva. I also worked close with Prof. Marcel Campen and Prof. Denis Zorin.
